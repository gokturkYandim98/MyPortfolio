Industrial Automation Project

With my classmates, we worked with Siemens PLC and Beckhoff Industrial Computer and learned Ladder programming. At the end, we created an autonomous assembly/production line which uses variety of components such as optical, metal sensors, pneumatic pistons,  relays and chain/conveyor belts. We also used variety of power tools, made cable connections and used variety of structural materials to create the production line.​​Our project worked flawlessly in all tests and won the best grade among other groups.
