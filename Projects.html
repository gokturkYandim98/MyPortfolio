Expense Tracker

My first IOS application that I made as a practice. Expense Tracker helps you to save and keep track of your daily income and expenses. You can create your own custom categories and assign it to different income/expense types. You can also use the graph tab in the App to see how much you have spent or received in different categories.

Industrial Automation Project

With my classmates, we worked with Siemens PLC and Beckhoff Industrial Computer and learned Ladder programming. At the end, we created an autonomous assembly/production line which uses variety of components such as optical, metal sensors, pneumatic pistons,  relays and chain/conveyor belts. We also used variety of power tools, made cable connections and used variety of structural materials to create the production line.

Vision Based Navigation of Arduino Robot

A project that I worked on during my Freshmen year in the Sabanci University. I designed a Arduino controlled circular robot using Solidworks and built it with the help of 3D printing and laser cutting. With my classmate, we combined a Raspberry Pi with the robot and a camera. We used OpenCV and color detection methods to navigate the robot autonomously.
