IOS Application

Expense Tracker

My first IOS application that I made as a practice. Expense Tracker helps you to save and keep track of your daily income and expenses. You can create your own custom categories and assign it to different income/expense types. You can also use the graph tab in the App to see how much you have spent or received in different categories.
